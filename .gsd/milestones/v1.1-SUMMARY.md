## Lessons Learned
- **Architecture**: Enforcing structural Joi validation at the router level prevents complex backend bugs.
- **Data Integrity**: Database triggers for `updated_at` must be religiously maintained to avoid cascade sync failures when rows are updated concurrently.
- **Concurrency**: When creating deferrable constraints, transaction blocks (`BEGIN`, `SET CONSTRAINTS ALL DEFERRED`, `COMMIT`) are critical for resolving race conditions in atomic swap operations.
- **Testing**: Unified controllers (like auth) require comprehensive endpoint testing aliasing (`/api/auth` vs `/api/auth/v2`) to ensure backward compatibility is preserved.
